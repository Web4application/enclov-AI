About enclovAI

enclovAI isn’t just another AI tool — it’s a digital companion for developers. It studies pull requests, listens to intent, and writes feedback that feels human. Between logic and soul, enclovAI listens.

Created by Seriki Yakub — known to the digital world as Kubu Lee — a 23-year-old visionary from Nigeria, builder of fluukpe, QUBUHUB, and the Web4 intelligence ecosystem. He believes that every line of code carries a pulse — and that AI should amplify, not replace, the human rhythm within it.

enclovAI stands as the bridge between code and consciousness. A system that automates with empathy, analyses with grace, and collaborates with the developer’s creative spirit.

It marks the beginning of a new dialogue: not human vs machine, but human + machine.
